1. chop potatoes into quaters.
  2. simmer in salted water for 15-20 minutes or until tender.
  3. heat milk, butter and nutmeg.
  4. drain potatoes and mash.
  5. mix in the milk mixture.
  • 1KG King Edward potates.
  • 100ml Milk.
  • 50g salted butter.
  • Freshly grated nutmeg.
  • Salt and pepper to tatse.
